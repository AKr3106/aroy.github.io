BEYOND THE CLASSROOM

LIST OF EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES:


At Kalyannagar Vidyapith, we believe in the overall development of our students, and hence, we offer a range of extracurricular activities such as sports, music, dance, drama, and debate. Our students have won several awards and accolades in these activities at the state and national level.

STUDENT INVOLVEMENT:


Our students are actively involved in extracurricular activities and are encouraged to pursue their interests and passions. We have a range of clubs and societies that students can join, such as the Science Club, the Eco Club, and the Debating Society.


BENEFITS OF PARTICIPATION:


Participating in extracurricular activities helps our students develop a range of skills such as leadership, teamwork, communication, and time management. It also helps them develop their creativity and confidence.
